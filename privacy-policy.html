Welcome to Dashboost. We operate dashboostconsulting.github.io (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy governs your use of our Service and explains how we collect, safeguard, and disclose information resulting from your use of our website.

2. Definitions

Service means the dashboostconsulting.github.io website operated by Dashboost.
Personal Data refers to data that can identify an individual.
Usage Data is data collected automatically from your interaction with the Service.
Cookies are small data files stored on your device.
Data Controller is the person or entity determining how personal data is processed. Dashboost is the Data Controller.
Data Processor is any person or service provider who processes data on behalf of the Data Controller.

User is the individual using our Service.

5. Use of Data

Dashboost uses the collected data to:
